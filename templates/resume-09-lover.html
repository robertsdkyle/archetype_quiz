— I — Essence

Every campaign should linger like a fragrance.

I work at the intersection of sensory design, emotional storytelling, and cultural intuition — building brands that don't simply sell, but seduce attention into something nearer to devotion.

My career has moved between Paris and New York, between fashion and fragrance, between the runway and the storefront. The constant has been a single instinct: the most enduring luxury is always personal — a brand made to feel as if it were waiting for you.

I take on a small number of houses each year, alongside selective consulting and editorial direction.
