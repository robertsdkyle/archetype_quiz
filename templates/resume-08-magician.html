✦ I · The Vision

Every problem contains a hidden door. My work is finding it.

I work at the threshold where technology, behavior, and imagination meet, turning organizations toward futures they could feel but couldn't yet name. The role keeps changing names: catalyst, architect, alchemist. The work doesn't.

Most transformation projects fail because they treat the surface and ignore the system. I begin where the system actually lives: in the rituals, incentives, and unspoken stories of the people inside it. Then I build outward, with technology as the spell, never the substance.
