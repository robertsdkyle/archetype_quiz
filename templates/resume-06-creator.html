— Who I Am

Great design is always a conversation between form and feeling.

I make things that didn't exist before — brand identities, editorial systems, motion, packaging. Across all of it, the work begins with a single question: what is this thing trying to say, and how would it say it if it were a person?

I trained as a graphic designer but the work has always pulled me wider — type, motion, color theory, a little 3D, occasionally code. I lead small teams toward cohesive output, and I write down the system as we build it.
